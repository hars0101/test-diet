Project Title

Swarm of Drones for Monitoring Wildlife Species

Problem Statement

Traditional wildlife monitoring methods endanger humans and animals while being inefficient. Our solution addresses the need for safe, non-invasive species tracking and data collection through autonomous drone swarms.

Solution Overview

Autonomous drone swarm features:

  • AI-powered species identification
  • Real-time data collection and reporting
  • Injury detection and population counting
  • Habitat mapping capabilities
  • Collaboration with conservation teams
